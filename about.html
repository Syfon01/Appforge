Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?

We serve a wide range of clients—from solo entrepreneurs and startups to large enterprises—across industries like finance, retail, tech, and education. Our solutions are tailored to suit each stage of growth.

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by complexity. A basic website or campaign may take 2–4 weeks, while custom software or infrastructure projects could take 6–12 weeks. You'll get a clear timeline after our initial consultation.

Can I request a custom package?

Yes. If your needs go beyond our listed services, we’ll work with you to design a custom package that aligns with your goals and budget.

What support do you provide after launch?

We offer post-launch support including bug fixes, updates, and technical assistance. Maintenance plans are also available for long-term reliability and performance.

Do you offer consultations before starting a project?

Definitely. We offer a free consultation to learn about your business and recommend the best solution before any commitment.
